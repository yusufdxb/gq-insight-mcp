[00:42] P-6675: The automation rules. I built a rule engine that auto-categorizes about eighty percent of our expenses by merchant and amount. What used to be a Monday-morning slog is now mostly done before I sit down.
[02:48] P-6675: The receipt matching is also underrated. It pairs card transactions to receipts automatically, and when it can't, it flags exactly what's missing instead of making me hunt.

[07:25] P-6675: And I wish the rules engine had version history. I tweak a rule, it misfires, and I can't see what I changed. For something this powerful, an undo would go a long way.
[09:40] Interviewer: If you were pitching Northwind to a peer, what's the one line?
[09:47] P-6675: "It does the boring 80 percent for you." That's the whole value. The automation is the moat — they should lean into it and fix reporting before someone out-automates them.
